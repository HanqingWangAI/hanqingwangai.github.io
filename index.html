About Me


Hi, I am Hanqing Wang - a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Media Computing and Intelligent Systems Laboratory at Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing. I am supervised by Prof. Wei Liang. In the past, I had the opportunity to collaborate with Prof. Lap-Fai(Craig) Yu, Director of the Design Computing and Extended Reality Group at George Mason University, Virginia.

From March to November 2017, I interned at Microsoft Research Asia in the VC Group, under the mentorship of researcher Jiaolong Yang. Subsequently, I was a research intern at the Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI) between July 2019 and April 2020, advised by Prof. Jianbing Shen, the laboratory's lead scientist.
